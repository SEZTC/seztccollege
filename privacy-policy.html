REGISTRATION GUIDE

The collge does not distribute or reveal any applicant information for marketing purposes or any other purpose that risks putting student privacy at risk.

Upon successfull registration the applicant will receive a email from the school requesting for O LEVEL results and (if available) A LEVEL results as well.


Applicant will also need to present pdf of national identification as well as digital copy of birth certificate.

After successfully submitting all required documents to the school email the candidate will be notified by email within 72 hours.

  • Since this will be submitted with other documents during initial registration the student may be required to produce another copy of their national ID
