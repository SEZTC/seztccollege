ABOUT SEZTC

A christian college that offers the best opportunities for those looking to learn, progress and succeed in their respective fields. Located in Mashonaland west in Zimbabwe the technical school which is registered with the Ministry of Higher and Tertiary Education awards both the National foundation certificate(NFC) and the National certificate (NC) to those who have succesfully completed their courses.

SEZTC offers a variety of different HEXCO courses that applicants can do after enrolling at the college with the mimimum qualifications being at least one O Level pass for the National Foundation Certificate. As a christian institution the college also instills proper values and principles that make students there better people after completing their respective courses as the school motto says Christianity Ensures Academic Excellence thus the college develops its students to such a extent that they embody the will of the school to achieve and display excellence as a result of proper guidence.
